- **Prompts** — Templates used for lead email generation, classification, and extraction
- **Agents** — Autonomous AI configurations for multi-step decision making
- **RAG** — Retrieval pipelines for semantic knowledge base systems
- **Embeddings** — Vectorization strategies for similarity search
